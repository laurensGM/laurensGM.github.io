Jobboard - Driving Efficiency: Transforming Manual Processes into Streamlined Workflows

  • Laurens Goormachtigh
  • 0 to 1

About Studysmarter

StudySmarter is a digital learning platform that provides tools and resources to help students with their studies. StudySmarter offers features such as collaborative learning, digital flashcards, study plans, and access to a vast library of educational materials. The platform aims to support students in organizing their study materials, creating effective study plans, and collaborating with peers.

In order for Studysmarter to offer the study platform for free to its users, it collaborated with companies and universities who aim to recruit the students. Hence the companies shared their job advertisements on the platform. We had many pages with company profiles and job details pages published on our website. However, creating those company profiles and job listings happened manually by internal staff. This was a slow, manual process with a lot of back and forth between internal staff and customers.

My role

As a seasoned Senior Product Manager, I thrive on solving complex challenges and enhancing user experiences. In my role at StudySmarter, I played a pivotal role in revolutionizing our job board, demonstrating my ability to drive efficiency and streamline operations. Our job board featured company profiles and job details, but the process of creating and managing these listings was slow and cumbersome. The manual involvement of internal staff led to inefficiencies, causing extended turnaround times and frequent back-and-forths with customers. Recognizing the need for improvement, my objective was clear: enhance the workflow, making it more efficient for both our internal team and our users who were seeking to create or edit profiles and job listings.

To address this challenge, I orchestrated a transition, moving the creation and management of profiles and jobs away from Django and into a purpose-built Jobboard. Taking a strategic approach, I conducted a comprehensive business case study and user story mapping exercise, examining the process from both the student and employer perspectives.

Outcomes

The implementation of Jobboard brought about transformative changes. Companies could now independently create and edit their company profiles and job listings, eliminating the need for extensive manual involvement from our internal staff. The time spent per company profile saw a remarkable reduction, plummeting from 20 minutes to a mere 5 minutes. This efficiency improvement of 4x not only saved valuable time but also greatly enhanced the overall user experience. Key Achievements Successfully transitioned the creation and management of profiles and jobs to Jobboard. Conducted a thorough business case study and user story mapping exercise. Achieved a 4x improvement in efficiency, reducing the time spent per company profile from 20 minutes to 5 minutes. Takeaways This project exemplifies my strategic thinking, ability to drive impactful changes, and commitment to enhancing user experiences. As a Senior Product Manager, I am passionate about leveraging technology to streamline processes and deliver solutions that make a lasting impact.
