Strategic Content Mastery: Elevating NPS and Fueling Chessable's Course Catalog Growth

  • Laurens Goormachtigh
  • Growth

About Chessable

Chessable is an online platform dedicated to chess learning. It offers a variety of courses and training materials for chess players of all skill levels. The platform utilizes a unique approach to learning chess, incorporating principles of spaced repetition and interactive training.

Creating a course on Chessable was a painful and time consuming process.

My role

As a Product Manager at Chessable, I spearheaded a transformative initiative to overhaul the course creation process on our platform, collaborating closely with the head of design and cross-functional teams. Recognizing the crucial role of user feedback, we conducted extensive interviews with content creators, editors, quality assurance teams, and coaches involved in crafting chess courses. Our approach involved categorizing existing tools into Export, Editing Variations, and Import tools.

To gauge the effectiveness of these tools, we implemented a Net Promoter Score (NPS) measurement, which we administered twiceâ€”first in June 2020 and then in September 2020. The results were staggering; we witnessed a substantial increase in the NPS for the content creation team, soaring from 3.5 to an impressive 6.8 within a span of just four months.

This project showcases my ability to drive positive change through strategic product management, collaboration, and a user-centric approach. It exemplifies how a thoughtful redesign of tools can not only enhance user satisfaction but also translate into substantial business growth and international expansion.

Outcomes

This improvement had a cascading impact on Chessable's performance metrics. The enhanced tools not only facilitated an accelerated publishing schedule but also contributed to an extraordinary 132% year-over-year growth in revenue. The content creation team, once comprised of 7 individuals, burgeoned to 32 members, a testament to the efficacy of our improvements.

Furthermore, the number of courses published surged, reflecting the growing confidence of users in utilizing the revamped content creation tools. Notably, even top-tier chess grandmasters embraced these tools, elevating the professionalism of the platform. The success story extended globally as we expanded into new markets, introducing Chessable in Spanish and German, broadening our reach and impact.
